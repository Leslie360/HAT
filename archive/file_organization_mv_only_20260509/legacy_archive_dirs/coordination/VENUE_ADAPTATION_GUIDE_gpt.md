# Venue Adaptation Guide: Submission Framing for Tier-B Candidates

针对战略重置后的多元化投稿目标，本项目拟定了针对 **npj Computational Materials** 和 **Advanced Intelligent Systems** 的适配方案。

---

## 候选 A: npj Computational Materials (Nature 体系)
**定位**: 偏向于跨尺度建模、计算驱动的材料评估。

### 1. 摘要侧重 (Abstract Micro-edits)
- **增加**: 强调 "Cross-scale bridge from microscopic device laws to macroscopic inference accuracy"。
- **减弱**: 减弱对 "Vision Transformer architecture engineering" 的技术细节描述。
- **标题建议**: *Cross-Scale Behavioral Simulation of Organic Optoelectronic Synaptic Networks for In-Memory Vision*

### 2. Cover Letter Framing
> "Our work establishes a direct computational link between the stochastic conductance dynamics of organic semiconductors and the deployment risk of modern AI backbones. By ingesting literature-derived device profiles, we provide materials scientists with a predictive threshold for device-to-device variability and retention drift, essential for guiding the next generation of materials design."

---

## 候选 B: Advanced Intelligent Systems (Wiley)
**定位**: 偏向于 AI 系统、类脑计算、边缘智能应用。

### 1. 摘要侧重 (Abstract Micro-edits)
- **增加**: 强调 "Robust edge deployment" 和 "Ensemble HAT as a mitigation for hardware-specific non-idealities"。
- **减弱**: 减弱对 "gradient-scaling approximations" 等物理建模细节的探讨。
- **标题建议**: *Enabling Robust Edge-Vision via Profile-Driven Organic Optoelectronic CIM Simulation*

### 2. Cover Letter Framing
> "We present an end-to-end framework for evaluating vision transformer deployment on organic optoelectronic hardware. Our core contribution is Ensemble HAT, a training strategy that overcomes hardware-instance overfitting, enabling zero-shot transfer to fresh hardware. This work bridges the gap between emerging organic synapses and scalable intelligent system deployment at the edge."
