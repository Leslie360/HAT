## **Prompt 3: Why Dynamic Attention Stays Digital**

### #1: Hemlet: Heterogeneous CIM Chiplet for ViTs (最强)

**Citation**: "Hemlet: A Heterogeneous Compute-in-Memory Chiplet Architecture for Vision Transformers," 2025.[^15]

**DOI**: Available via Themoonlight.io review

**Key Design Conclusion**:
- **ACIM chiplet (RRAM)**: 静态权重 (W_Q, W_K, W_V, W_O)
- **DCIM chiplet (SRAM)**: 动态 attention (QK^T, Softmax, PV)
- **原因**: Dynamic VMMs 需要频繁更新，SRAM write endurance 优于 eNVM

**Supports**: **Keeping attention digital (SRAM-based DCIM)**

**Manuscript Sentence**:
"Heterogeneous CIM architectures partition ViTs such that static-weight linear projections (W_Q, W_K, W_V, W_O) map to analog RRAM crossbars, while dynamic attention computations (QK^T, Softmax, PV) are offloaded to SRAM-based digital CIM due to frequent updates and write endurance constraints."[^15]

## **总结与建议**

### **最推荐的引用策略**

#### **Optical Crosstalk**
- **优先引用**: Zhang et al. (your case-study), ACS AMI 2023[^3][^1]
- **关键点**: 明确说"electrical crosstalk can be eliminated via patterning, but **optical crosstalk** (illumination uniformity, pixel-to-pixel interference) remains a challenge in organic arrays"

#### **IR Drop & Sneak Path**
- **优先引用**: Fast IR-Drop Model, ICON-NIV, Sneak Path Modeling[^7][^8][^10]
- **6-bit ADC 支撑**: 结合 IR drop 讨论，说明"6-bit ADC balances quantization noise and IR-drop-induced errors"

#### **ViT Attention**
- **优先引用**: Hemlet (heterogeneous ACIM/DCIM split), HyAtten (photonic-digital hybrid)[^15][^18]
- **关键句**: "Dense static projections (W_Q, W_K, W_V, W_O) map well to analog arrays, but dynamic attention products (QK^T, Softmax, PV) remain digital or specialized mixed-signal due to write endurance and nonlinearity constraints"

#### **Temperature Sensitivity**
- **优先引用**: Fuller et al. Science Advances 2020 (ion gel stability), Guo et al. Adv. Mater. 2024 (high-temp phototransistors)[^22][^23]
- **关键句**: "Organic optoelectronic devices exhibit temperature-sensitive threshold voltage, mobility, and retention, with conductance drift accelerated at elevated temperatures due to enhanced ion migration and structural disorder"
