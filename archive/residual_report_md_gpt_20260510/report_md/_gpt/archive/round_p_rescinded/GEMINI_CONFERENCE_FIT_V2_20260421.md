## Full Venue Assessment

| Venue | Fit | 1-Sentence Rationale | Est. Acceptance Rate | Reviewer Pool Values |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Nature Electronics | 5 | Publishes high-impact physical-boundary and negative-result stories if framed as paradigm-shifting insight. | ~8–12 % | Fundamental physics, cross-disciplinary impact, novelty of question |
| NeurIPS (main) | 4 | ML theory track explicitly welcomes impossibility results and scaling-limit proofs. | ~20–25 % | Rigor, reproducibility, impact on algorithm design |
| NeurIPS-Hardware | 3 | Audience wants hardware–algorithm co-design wins; negative result needs strong positive takeaway. | ~18–22 % | Practical speedups, benchmark gains, deployability |
| ICML | 3 | Relevant theory track, but main pool is optimization-success oriented; harder sell. | ~22–27 % | Empirical improvement, theoretical clarity, novelty |
| MLSys | 2 | Systems community prioritizes working systems and measured speedups over limit proofs. | ~18–23 % | End-to-end performance, reproducibility artifacts |
| IEDM | 2 | Device-engineering forum; a circuit-level limit paper is out of scope unless tied to a new device physics story. | ~25–30 % | Device innovation, process integration, experimental data |
| ISSCC | 1 | Demands working silicon and clear performance advantage; negative results are virtually unpublishable. | ~20–25 % | Measured silicon metrics, power, area, speed |
| DATE | 3 | Design-automation community tolerates methodology and analysis papers that redefine feasibility regions. | ~22–26 % | Modeling rigor, design-space insight, tool relevance |
| DAC | 3 | Similar to DATE; methodology-heavy limit studies can find a home in the right technical track. | ~20–24 % | Scalability, algorithmic insight, EDA relevance |

## Bottom-Line Recommendation

**Target first:**
1. **Nature Electronics** — highest fit for a fundamental-limits narrative and carries the broadest interdisciplinary credibility.
2. **NeurIPS (main track)** — the ML theory community is uniquely socialized to treat falsification as a first-class contribution; strongest alignment with the paper’s intellectual core.

*Fallback tier:* DATE or DAC if the story can be reframed as design-space methodology rather than pure physical limit.
