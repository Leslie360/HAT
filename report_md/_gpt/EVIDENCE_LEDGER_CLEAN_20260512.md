## Evidence Grade Definitions

- **claim-bearing**: Has source data, reproducibility metadata, and is the canonical source for a main claim. Can cite in paper/thesis.
- **candidate**: Strong supporting evidence but not yet the canonical source for a main claim. Can cite with caveats.
- **provisional**: Useful pilot/audit/supporting evidence; do not make central claims.
- **diagnostic**: Implementation check or sanity test; do not use as narrative evidence.
- **invalid**: Retained for provenance only; superseded by corrected result.
